TinyOwl Technology Pvt. Ltd.

Co-Founder• May 2013 - October 2016

TinyOwl is a Mumbai-based company founded by IIT Bombay Alumni in 2013. We were a team of designers, developers and strategists backed by Sequoia Capital, Nexus Ventures and Matrix Ventures and mentored by Sandeep Tandon, Kunal Shah(Founders of Freecharge), Kunal Bahl, Rohit Bansal(Founders of Snapdeal). Our mission was to connect people with their food. We introduced concept of Homemade food - where users can order food made by house-wives at home.

Backend Technology - Headed team of 22 engineers to develop and maintain robust and scalable backend to support TinyOwl application. Started with Monolith and later migrated to Microservies which could handle upto 100k orders a day. Implemented on Ruby on Rails with PostgreSQL and MongoDB database, RabbitMQ for async request handling and Redis for caching.

Growth - Assembled and Headed team of 2 Developers, 1 Designer and 2 Business Analysts to work on 2 most important metrics of the company - User Acquisition and Retention. Re-engineered Referral engine and improved users acquired through referrals from 50% to 80%. This reduced Cost of Acquisition(CAC) by 45%. For Retention, we developed a system to send smart and personalized notifications at the right time and based on the taste buds of the users. We tested, analysed, re-iterated and found interesting insights like users mostly ordered pizzas for Friday Dinner, tendency to click on notification almost doubles if image is present and many more.
